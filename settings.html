Help and support
How does ShapeCue update my plan?
ShapeCue uses your onboarding details, completed meals and workouts, exercise reports, pain logs, measurements, and recent plan history. Your current plan remains active while a new plan is being generated.
What is the difference between Premium and Coach?
Premium updates every 14 days and includes detailed meals, exercise media, limited progress photos, and a basic progress review. Coach updates weekly and adds full smart replacements, photo AI review, Progress Vault PIN, advanced weekly review, and explanations for why your plan changed.
Are my progress photos private?
Progress photos are stored in a private Supabase bucket with per-user access rules. ShapeCue should only use them for AI review when you explicitly allow it. Avoid including your face.
Does ShapeCue replace medical advice?
No. ShapeCue provides general fitness and wellness guidance. It does not diagnose conditions, prescribe treatment, or replace a doctor, physiotherapist, registered dietitian, or other qualified professional.
